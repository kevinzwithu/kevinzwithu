## About

I am a full-stack developer working across frontend, backend, DevOps, and product-oriented engineering.

Most of my professional work happens on GitHub Enterprise, so it is not reflected on this public profile. The repositories here are mainly personal projects, experiments, and selected side work.

Some of my more important personal projects are intentionally kept private to protect their users and reduce the risk of misuse. I care more about building things that work quietly and sustainably than making every piece of work public.

I rarely contribute PRs to open-source projects. As code generation and vulnerability discovery become increasingly accessible, I prefer to spend my limited energy on my own small world: building, writing, composing, and maintaining the things I personally care about.
